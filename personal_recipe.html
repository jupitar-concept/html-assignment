How to Make Chin Chin

Back to my Profile

Chin Chin is a popular crunchy snack in Nigeria. This simple recipe will guide you through making delicious homemade chin chin for personal use or business.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter or Margarine
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Nutmeg (optional)
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and nutmeg in a bowl.
  2. Add butter and mix until crumbly.
  3. Add eggs and milk, then mix into a dough.
  4. Roll out the dough on a flat surface.
  5. Cut into small cubes or desired shapes.
  6. Heat oil in a pan and fry until golden brown.
  7. Remove and allow to cool before serving.
